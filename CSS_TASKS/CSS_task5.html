About AI Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are designed to think, learn, and make decisions. At its core, AI uses algorithms and data to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence, such as problem-solving, decision-making, speech recognition, and understanding natural language. Over time, AI systems can improve themselves through experience, a process known as machine learning. AI has become a major force across industries, transforming the way we live and work. In healthcare, AI powers diagnostic tools and personalized medicine. In business, it drives automation, customer service chatbots, and data-driven decision-making. Entertainment, finance, education, and even agriculture are leveraging AI to enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and create smarter solutions.
